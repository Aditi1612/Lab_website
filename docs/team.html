About the Principal Investigator

Dr. Diwakar Tiwari is a Senior Professor at the Department of Chemistry, Mizoram University. He is Director of the DRDO-Industry-Academia Centre of Excellence (DIA-COE) and the Internal Quality Assurance Cell (IQAC). His research group works at the interface of environmental chemistry, materials science, and electrochemistry.
