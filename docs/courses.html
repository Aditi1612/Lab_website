About Teaching

Dr. Diwakar Tiwari has over 25 years of teaching experience at the postgraduate and doctoral level in the Department of Chemistry, Mizoram University. His teaching spans core analytical and environmental chemistry topics, bridging fundamental theory with current research challenges in water treatment and environmental remediation.
