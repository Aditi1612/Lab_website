Research Focus

Areas of Excellence

Our interdisciplinary research addresses critical environmental challenges through cutting-edge chemistry and materials science.

Advanced Oxidation Processes

Fenton and Photo-Fenton-like processes for remediation of water and wastewater. Development of heterogeneous catalysts with superior degradation efficiency for emerging micropollutants.

Fenton Catalysis Wastewater Treatment Micropollutants

Electrochemical Sensors

Development of highly sensitive electrochemical sensors for trace detection of heavy metals, pharmaceuticals, and micropollutants in aquatic environments using nanocomposite materials.

Heavy Metal Detection Nanocomposites Trace Analysis

Photocatalysis

Solar and UV-driven photocatalytic degradation of organic pollutants using semiconductor heterojunctions, noble metal decorated photocatalysts, and S-scheme/Z-scheme systems.

TiO₂ Photocatalysts Solar Driven Heterojunctions

Functional Nanomaterials

Design and synthesis of bimetallic nanocatalysts, MOFs, clay composites, graphene oxide hybrids, carbon dots, and polymer-based membranes for environmental and energy applications.

MOFs Graphene Oxide Nanofiltration
